OUR DIGITAL WORLD IS FILTERED

The world that we see is sometimes filtered through the lens of our internet search engines or App newsfeeds. Each of us gets different results when we search for key terms, depending on our backgrounds, views, and history. See how "they" see the world by comparing your search results against theirs for any phrase. Submit a search term that you are curious about and wish to compare to others.
